### Trust and Control: Your Wallet

Alta is built on the principles of security and user control. When you use Alta, you stay in control of your assets through a **self-custodial** approach. This means your holdings are secured by technology that you verify, not just a platform you are asked to believe in.

**How it works**:

- **Privacy and Security**: Alta uses modern infrastructure to ensure that your money stays yours.
- **Verified Access**: Under the hood, Alta utilizes self-custodial mechanics to ensure transparency and direct control over your funds.
