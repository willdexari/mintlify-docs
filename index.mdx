### Key Features

**Predict Up or Down** Alta uses literal language to keep you in control. If you think a market will rise, **Predict Up**. If you think it will fall, **Predict Down**. When you are finished, simply **Cash out**.

**Boost Your Conviction** When your conviction is high, you can **Boost** your prediction to increase your potential upside. Boost lets you increase your upside by up to 50x. This is an optional choice that amplifies results. Remember that losses amplify too.

**The Climb** Alta is built like a climb where every action moves you forward.

- **XP & Levels**: Earn XP through activity to reach new levels.
- **Unlocks**: Higher levels unlock more features and access.
- **Credits**: These are non-withdrawable units used to place predictions.
- **Gems**: Collect in-app items used for special actions.

**Trust & Control** We believe in access you can verify. Alta is built on security and transparency. This ensures you stay in control with clear outcomes for every action.
