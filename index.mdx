Alta is an app where you **add cash**, **predict Up or Down** on markets, and **cash out** when you are done. You can also use **Boost** to increase exposure on a prediction.

## Start here

**How Alta works**\
Learn the core flow: add cash → choose a market → pick **Up** or **Down** → optional **Boost** → **Cash Out**.

**Is Alta available in my region?**\
See supported countries and what features are currently **Locked** or **Unlocked**.

## Key terms

- **Up / Down**: Your direction prediction on a market.
- **Boost**: Increases your exposure on a prediction.
- **Cash Out**: Closes your prediction.

## Explore markets

- **Stocks**: Price exposure to popular public companies.
- **Pre-IPO markets**: Price exposure to select private-market assets.
- **Indexes**: Broad market benchmarks in a single market.
- **Crypto**: Crypto market categories available in Alta.
- **Metals & Energy**: Real asset markets like metals and energy.
