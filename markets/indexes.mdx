## What you can do with Indexes

- Browse available index markets
- Predict **Up** or **Down**
- Optional: use **Boost** to increase exposure
- **Cash Out** anytime to close your prediction

## What to know

- Indexes can move quickly, especially during periods of high market volatility.
- Availability of Indexes can vary by region and may change over time.
