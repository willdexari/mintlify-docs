## What you can do with Pre-IPO markets

- Browse available Pre-IPO markets
- Predict **Up** or **Down**
- Optional: use **Boost** to increase exposure
- **Cash Out** anytime to close your prediction

## Important note

Pre-IPO markets in Alta are designed for price exposure. They are not the same as owning shares in a private company.

## What to know

- Availability of Pre-IPO markets can vary by region and may change over time.
- These markets may have higher volatility than other categories.
