## The core flow

1. **Add cash**\
   Fund your Alta account using the options available in your region.
2. **Choose a market**\
   Browse categories like **Stocks**, **Private markets**, **Indexes**, **Crypto**, and **Metals & Energy**.
3. **Pick Up or Down**\
   Select the direction you think the market price will move.
4. **Optional: use Boost**\
   Turn on **Boost** if you want more exposure on your prediction.
5. **Cash Out when you’re done**\
   Cash Out closes your prediction and realizes your result.

## Key terms

- **Market**: An asset or category you can make a prediction on.
- **Up / Down**: Your direction prediction on the market price.
- **Boost**: Increases your exposure on a prediction.
- **Cash Out**: Closes your prediction.

## Important notes

- Markets can move quickly and outcomes are not guaranteed.
- Availability of markets and features can vary by region.
